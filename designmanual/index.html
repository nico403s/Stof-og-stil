Design Manual

Primary Logo

Stoff og Stil's logo is primarily used in a certain place in the top right or top left corner. It'll never be seen towards the bottom of the screen as that space is reserved for usable buttons.

In the app, the logo must not be usable, as it is only used for show.

Tone Of Voice

Happy

- Sells by speaking good of the products with a choice of words that resembles happy and refreshing feelings.
- Extrovert and young/fresh.
- Is familiar to the customer.
- All things can be exciting.
- Is capable of seeing posibilities in everything.

Calm

- Sells by keeping everything on a level where everyone can understand what's going on.
- Describing, calm/slow tempo.
- "Like Grandma used to do it".
- Easy to understand. Controlled use of slang.
- Is very feeling, in the way that pathos means a lot.

Professional

- Sells by speaking to the Ethos and Logos of the customer.
- Number focused (prices and such).
- A bit shorter in sentences. More straight to the point.
- Very showing. Makes the important things very noticable.
- Has experience in all areas within the subject.
- Is very precise with the information given back.
